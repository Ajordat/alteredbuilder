It's finally possible to import unique cards!
Import your unique cards into the deck builder and add them into your decks!

  • {% translate "New features:" %}
    • New system to Follow users: Subscribe to your favorite deck creators so you're notified whenever they create a new deck
    • Added user profiles
      • List of users (most recent, most viewed and most followed) accessible from the tab " {% translate "Builders" %}"
      • Profile page for each user displaying a custom bio, public decks and its faction distribution. For example: Equinox
      • List of followers and following users
      • Access the profile of a deck's creator by clicking on their username
    • Functionality to import unique cards. Take the reference of the unique cards you like and import them into the deck building!
    • Functionality to search individual cards by their reference. This helps greatly finding specific unique cards: ref:ALT_COREKS_B_OR_21_U_2139.
      • When filtering by reference any other filtering parameters will be ignored.
  • {% translate "Other changes:" %}
    • The notifications are now retrieved strictly when opening the dropdown. It used to be rendered along the whole page, but this method grants better performance.
    • When viewing the detail of a deck, the card display is now kept on screen while scrolling through the page.
