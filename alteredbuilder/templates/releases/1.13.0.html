Quality of Life changes

The release 1.13.0 is finally here after I took some (arguably) well-deserved holidays.

First of all, to continue our efforts towards making this platform open-source and collaborative with a wider audience, we've integrated it with Weblate.
Weblate is an open-source project that allows to submit a project's strings of characters so that people can work together translating them to other languages. Feel free to check it out!

This update also brings a series of Quality of Life improvements that we're happy about. For example, adding a button to mark all notifications as read at once; filtering by faction when selecting a deck on the card's list or providing a way to edit a deck from the deck's view.

Lastly, I've linked Taum's site directly from the deck's view. This provides an alternative view of each deck that is more visually appealing and easier to share with others. The site is still under construction so currently it's not possible to make it work with unique cards or decks without a hero. Take a look a this example showcasing the Bravos starter deck.
